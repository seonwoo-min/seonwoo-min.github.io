Bio

Starting in Oct. 2023, I'm joining Amazon Web Services Health AI as an Applied Scientist.
I'm currently doing a Research Internship at Information Extraction Team, NAVER Cloud. I will be interning until September 8th.

Lately, I'm broadly interested in training, evaluating, and understanding trustworthy machine learning models (e.g. robustness, explainability, and uncertainty). Throughout my Ph.D. (2015-2021), my research interest was primarily at the intersection of machine learning and computational biology. I worked on a few challenging problems such as CRISPR activity prediction, training of protein language models, and abnormality identification from ECGs.

I was a research scientist at Fundamental Research Laboratory, LG AI Research for 1.2 years. I received my Ph.D. at Data Science & Artificial Intelligence Laboratory from Seoul National University in 2021. I received B.S. in Electrical and Computer Engineering from Seoul National University in 2015. I spent two summers doing internships at NAVER CLOVA in 2019 and NAVER Cloud in 2023. I was a recipient of the BK21 Plus Outstanding Researcher Award in 2020, the Microsoft Research Asia Fellowship Nomination Award in 2018, and the NRF Global Ph.D. Fellowship in 2016-2020.
